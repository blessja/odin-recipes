Banana Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted
  • Optional: maple syrup or fresh fruit for topping
  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the mashed bananas, flour, baking powder, salt, milk, and egg. Mix until well combined.
  3. In a greased 9x13 inch baking dish, evenly distribute the banana batter. Sprinkle the melted butter on top.
  4. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  5. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy with your favorite toppings!
