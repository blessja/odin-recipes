Cheesecake

Our best cheesecake recipe, made with a creamy and rich base and topped with a luscious chocolate ganache.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 1/2 cups cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  1. Combine graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter in a bowl. Press mixture into the bottom and sides of a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. Bake crust for 8 minutes, then cool on a wire rack.
  3. Beat cream cheese and sugar in a bowl until smooth. Pour over crust and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until center is almost set. Cool cheesecake on a wire rack for at least 2 hours before serving.
  4. For the ganache, melt chocolate in a double boiler. Pour over cheesecake and refrigerate until set. Enjoy!
  5. For a delicious twist, top with fresh berries or a dollop of whipped cream.
